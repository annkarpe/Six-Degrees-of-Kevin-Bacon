Six Degrees of Kevin Bacon

Six Degrees of Kevin Bacon

is a parlor game that highlights the concept of interconnectedness in the entertainment industry. The game challenges participants to connect any actor to Kevin Bacon within six or fewer steps, based on shared film roles. This idea originated from the observation that Kevin Bacon has worked with a diverse range of actors across various genres. It emphasizes the notion that most actors in Hollywood can be linked through a relatively small number of connections, showcasing the interconnected nature of the film industry.
